About chseets

chseets is a free, open source platform for creating and sharing printable cheat sheets.

Whether you need quick reference guides for travel, study, or work, chseets provides a simple way to access and share knowledge.

What We Cover

Our cheat sheets are designed with two main focuses:

Travel and Tourism

  • City guides and points of interest
  • Regional overviews and travel tips
  • Country essentials (visa, currency, phrases)
  • Transportation and navigation aids

Education and Professions

  • Study guides for students
  • Quick reference cards for professionals
  • Technical documentation summaries
  • Language learning aids

Why chseets?

  • Always accessible: Works offline as a PWA
  • Print ready: Optimized for A4 printing
  • Community driven: Upload and share your own sheets
  • Privacy first: No tracking, no ads
  • Open source: Free forever, contribute on GitHub

Open Source
