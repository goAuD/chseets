About chseets

chseets is a lightweight open-source project for creating and sharing printable cheat sheets across multiple technical fields.

It started as part of the WASD Travel ecosystem - designed for students, developers, educators, museums, citys and travelers who want quick, portable references without clutter or ads.

The goal: clean design, open contribution, and fully offline access through Progressive Web App (PWA) technology.

Vision

  • Universal format for short, high-quality study materials
  • Works fully offline (installable PWA)
  • Community-driven library of technical sheets
  • Privacy-first, no tracking

Maintained by WASD Travel • Contact: chseets@wasdtravel.com
