1. **Start - Hauptplatz Fountain**  
   Morning light, market chatter, coffee aroma. A good breath before the path.
2. **City Parish Church (St. Theresia)**  
   Baroque calm under soft bells. Notice the carved angels on the gate.
3. **Weizberg Hill & Pilgrimage Church**  
   A short climb, quiet benches, panoramic view over red roofs.  
   _Optional quest: trace the old stone cross path._
4. **Taborkaserne Tower (Old Fortress)**  
   Medieval traces meet 20th-century walls. The sound here is mostly wind.
5. **River Raab Riverside Path**  
   Cool air, willow leaves, cyclists passing. Perfect closing stretch.  

> _Tip:_ total length ~4 km - slow pace: 1.5 h including photos & pauses.
